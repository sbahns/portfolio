About Me

I am currently working as a freelance front end web developer / UX designer. I've been in this field for over 20 years developing websites for small businesses, higher education, events, state and federal government projects, non-profits, and programs.

The bulk of my work the last 6 years has been in working as part of a distributed team of contractors working for an agency out of Boston. So I am very comfortable in working remotely and as part of a distributed Scrum team. We use and using Git, Jira, Confluence, Slack, and Zoom to collaborate.

The vast majority of my work is in developing custom themes and collaborating on plugins for WordPress, but I have worked with other platforms as well. I am a strong team player and am well-regarded for my ability to get things done, meet deadlines, and coordinate many types of projects.

My skillset includes UI/UX, HTML, CSS/SCSS, Sass, PHP, WordPress, JavaScript, jQuery, Git, responsive frameworks (Foundation, Bootstrap), the Atlassian suite of tools (Jira, Confluence, Tempo, etc.), BaseCamp, Asana, Plesk, cPanel, and other hosting admin tools, XML, W3C standards, Section 508 ADA standards.

I also have experience with teaching, print layout, graphic design, writing, campaigns and other public relations-related skills.
